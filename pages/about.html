Surabhi Balachander grew up in Tippecanoe County, Indiana, and graduated from Stanford University in 2017 with a B.A. in English and Comparative Studies in Race and Ethnicity. From 2017-18, she worked at the Bill Lane Center for the American West at Stanford University. Surabhi is currently a PhD student in English Language and Literature at the University of Michigan.
